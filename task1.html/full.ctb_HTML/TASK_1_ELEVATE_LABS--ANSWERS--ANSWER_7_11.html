WE Have discussed some of them in ANSWER_6 , but the main risks are related to these ports :-

| Port | Service | Risk Summary

| --------- | --------- | --------------------------------------------

| 21 | FTP | Insecure, allows clear-text login

| 23 | Telnet | Outdated, insecure shell

| 139/445 | SMB | Prone to exploits like EternalBlue

| 2049 | NFS | Unprotected file shares

| 5900 | VNC | Often no authentication, screen control risk

| 1099 | Java RMI | Remote code execution

| 3306/5432 | Databases | SQL injection, data leakage if misconfigured

| 5000 | UPnP | Remote device control, often exploited .
